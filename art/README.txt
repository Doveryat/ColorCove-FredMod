ColorCove: An indie-game by DillyzThe1 for a mini-game-jam.

Hey there! Welcome to ColorCove!
A game where you find all the dummies not having fun in a crowd of random business men.
Before you ask, yes, I will be adding more content and characters in the future.
All project files are accessible and easily moddable through the github page.
